✅ 5 Do's

Keep your profile photo and bio professional on platforms like LinkedIn
Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with your professor seeing it
Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal content
Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas
Report harassment or harmful content using the platform's report feature

❌ 5 Don'ts

Don't share your phone number, home address, or college ID publicly
Don't post during exam stress or anger — you may regret it later
Don't share copyrighted material (songs, films) without permission
Don't accept friend/follow requests from people you don't know
Don't spread unverified news or forwards — always check the source first
