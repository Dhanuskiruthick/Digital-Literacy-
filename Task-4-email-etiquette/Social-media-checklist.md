# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students
## CSE0001 Digital Literacy | VIT Bhopal University
### Dhanus Kiruthick A | 25MEI10018

---

## Do's

- [ ] Keep your profile photo and bio professional on platforms 
      like LinkedIn and GitHub
- [ ] Think before you post — ask yourself if you would be 
      comfortable with your professor or parents seeing it
- [ ] Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal 
      content on Instagram and Facebook
- [ ] Give proper credit when sharing someone else's work, 
      ideas, or images online
- [ ] Report harassment, hate speech, or harmful content using 
      the platform's built-in report feature

---

## Don'ts

- [ ] Don't share your phone number, home address, or college ID 
      publicly on any social media platform
- [ ] Don't post during moments of anger, stress, or exam 
      pressure — you may deeply regret it later
- [ ] Don't share copyrighted material such as songs, films, or 
      book content without proper permission
- [ ] Don't accept friend or follow requests from people you 
      don't personally know or recognize
- [ ] Don't spread unverified news or forwards — always verify 
      the source before sharing anything

---

*Remember: What goes online, stays online — forever.*
